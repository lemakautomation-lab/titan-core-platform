# BD-006 – AI Architecture

**Document ID:** BD-006

**Status:** Approved

**Version:** 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines the Artificial Intelligence architecture used throughout the TITAN Core Platform.

The AI Engine is a shared platform service available to every TITAN application.

---

# Vision

Create a central AI platform that provides intelligent assistance, automation and decision support across all TITAN products.

---

# AI Design Principles

- AI is a shared platform service.
- AI assists users but does not replace human decisions.
- AI interactions are auditable.
- AI services are modular and reusable.
- Privacy and security are built into every AI feature.
